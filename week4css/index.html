Felix Lin's Portfolio

Bio

Meet Felix Lin, a passionate communication design student based in New
York City. With a keen eye for detail and a love for visual storytelling, Felix
is dedicated to master the art of graphic design. Currently studying at New
York City College of Technology, Felix is exploring the intersection of creativity
and technology, honing skills in typography, branding, and user experience.

Self Portrait

This is my first self portrait I ever did using Adobe Illustrator. I was inspired
by Lois Mailou Jones painting “Initiation, Liberia”. Many of her artwork
reflected the influences of African history and culture so I took my own spin
on it by incorporating some components, patterns and design elements
that best represented me and my own cultural heritage into my portrait.

Package Design

For this project, I was tasked to redesign a package box of an existing skin
care product.This is the template of the package rebrand for the Daisy Marc
Jacobs perfume created using Adobe Illustrator. I wanted to rebrand it into
a more natural and vintage look compare to its original simplistic and
repetitive design by changing up the coloring and styling.

Mood Board

In this project, I created a vision board for 2022 using Adobe Photoshop as
a way to manifest our goals and visions in a visual way. In my vision board,
I filled it with images that represent my future goals including things I
want and want to achieve for the year.
